History of Alfaleads

2016
a team of like-minded people founded Alfaleads concluded the first partnerships and integrated the first offers into our database reached the first million in payouts to our affiliates
2017
we gained steam — the team was 4 times bigger than at the beginning! moved to a modern spacious office created a media buying agency
2018
presented a Honda Shadow bike to the winner of the "Think and get Rich" promotion held CPACON — our own invite-only CPA conference organized many invite-only events for top players of the affiliate industry
2019
won the Affbank Awards in the "Best iGaming affiliate program" and "The most creative affiliate program" categories held a large-scale "Silicon fever" promotion with a trip to Silicon Valley as the main prize launched our own MBA educational program
2020
another milestone for our team — more than 200 employees at Alfaleads restricted the registration with the network to pay maximum attention to our affiliates finished the year with the "Beautiful Life" promotion and a large-scale prize draw: many gifts for our finalists and a Tesla Model S car for the lucky winner
2021
today, Alfaleads is a company with hundreds of thousands of affiliates worldwide, a fresh and strong team, and ambitious plans. We will not stop there and are ready for new achievements!
